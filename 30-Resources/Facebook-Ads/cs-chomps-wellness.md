# Chomps — Google-to-Meta Signal Loop

## Dữ Liệu Thực Tế

| Metric | Q1 2024 | Q1 2026 | Change |
|--------|---------|---------|--------|
| Blended ROAS | 3.2x | **5.1x** | ✅ +59% |
| CAC | Baseline | **-31%** | ✅ |
| Creative Iteration Cycle | >7 days | **<7 days** | ✅ |
| Spend Scale | $20M-$80M range | Tăng thêm | ✅ |

**Ngân sách:** $25K-$100K+/tháng  
**Industry:** Clean-ingredient meat stick / Better-for-you snack DTC  
**Goal:** Rebuild paid media từ siloed channels sang unified signal loop

---

## Problem Ban Đầu

- Optimizing 3 separate channels (Meta, Google, TikTok) like "three separate businesses"
- Attribution mess: last-click fiction, channel-level incentive misalignment
- iOS privacy erosion: ~65% buyers trên iPhone → platform-reported data structurally unreliable
- Creative organic performance on TikTok dying inside Advantage+ campaigns
- One creative Meta claimed "best performer for 6 weeks straight" → barely moving new customer acquisition (retargeting audience already won)

> "We were optimizing three separate channels like they were three separate businesses. The moment we started treating search intent as prospecting intelligence for Meta, everything changed."  
> — Keely Wolfsberg, VP of Growth

---

## Core Rebuild Components

### 1. Unified Measurement Stack
- **Northbeam** cho multi-touch attribution
- **Elevar** cho server-side tracking
- **KnoCommerce post-purchase survey** (34% completion rate) → single question: "where did you first hear about us?"
- Survey data = north star khi platform attribution disagree

### 2. Google-to-Meta Signal Loop
- Google search intent data → inform Meta prospecting audiences
- TikTok organic performance → feed paid creative selection
- Post-purchase surveys → close attribution gap

### 3. Channel Intelligence Findings
- TikTok organic = **#1 first-touch awareness driver under 35** (28% Q3 2025, zero paid spend)
- YouTube influenced **19% conversions aged 35-54** (almost none captured in Meta attribution)
- **41% buyers** saw Chomps ad trên ≥2 channels trước khi purchase → cross-channel exposure critical
- Google branded search volume growing **3x faster than paid search spend** → strong organic/social pull-through

## Kịch bản A: Google-to-Meta Signal Loop (Đã chứng minh)

**Setup:**
- Export high-intent Google search query data → build Meta custom audiences từ search term clusters
- TikTok organic top-performing content → adapt vào paid creative testing
- Post-purchase surveys (34% completion rate) → triangulate channel influence map

**Results:**
- Blended ROAS 3.2x → 5.1x
- CAC giảm 31%
- Creative iteration cycles <7 days

**Best for:** Brands đang chạy nhiều channels nhưng chưa integrate signal flow giữa chúng  
**Prerequisite:** Data team capable của export/search query analysis + custom audience building

## Kịch bản B: CBO Testing + ASC Scaling (Đã chứng minh)

**Setup:**
- Test creative trong **CBO structure** với tightly controlled audience/placement inputs
- Scale winners trong **ASC (Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns)**
- Minimum 6 creative concepts per 2-week sprint, mỗi concept cho specific audience hypothesis
- Không conflate testing và scaling environment

**Eric Melchor (Structured Agency):** "Advantage+ is a black box that rewards you handsomely for feeding it the right creative and punishes you invisibly for feeding it the wrong stuff. Most brands use it as a testing environment. That's backwards. You test in CBO, you scale in ASC."

**Prerequisite:** Budget ≥$500/ngày cho reliable testing (noise quá cao nếu <$200/ngày)  
**Best for:** Brands đã có conversion data đủ lớn để algorithm phân biệt winner/loser trong CBO

## Kịch bản C: Three Creative Tracks (Đã chứng minh)

**Setup — 3 tracks song song với cadence + metrics riêng:**

| Track | Format | Purpose | Audience |
|-------|--------|---------|----------|
| **Track 1: UGC-Native** | iPhone shot by contracted creators | Top funnel prospecting | Cold health-and-wellness audience |
| **Track 2: Founder-led** | Co-founder Pete Maldonado talking-head | Mid-funnel trust building | Warm audiences, consideration stage |
| **Track 3: Product-forward** | Built cho Google Performance Max | Bottom-funnel consideration | High-intent searchers |

**Key insight:** "Concept designed to convert TikTok-aware buyers who searched branded terms is structurally different from concept designed to introduce product to cold health-and-wellness audience." Stop trying to make one creative do both jobs.

---

## Bài Học Key

1. **Cross-channel signal flow > siloed channel optimization** — treat channels as signal generators for each other
2. **Test in CBO, scale in ASC** — conflate two environments = lose your read trên creative performance
3. **TikTok organic là free prospecting intelligence** — 28% first-touch awareness under 35 với zero paid spend
4. **Post-purchase survey data > platform attribution** khi có disagreement — trust the survey
5. **Separate creative cho mỗi job** — cold prospecting concept structurally different từ warm retargeting concept
