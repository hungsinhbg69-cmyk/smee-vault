# Caraway Home — Advantage+ Full Migration

## Dữ Liệu Thực Tế

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| ROAS | 2.9x | **4.6x** | ✅ +58% |
| CAC (New Customer) | Baseline | **-34%** | ✅ |
| 90-day LTV (Advantage+) | Manual benchmark | **+18%** | ✅ |
| Spend Scale | $2M+/tháng | Tăng thêm | ✅ |

**Ngân sách:** $3K-$25K/tháng  
**Industry:** Cookware DTC  
**Goal:** Rebuild Meta paid social architecture around Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns

---

## Problem Ban Đầu

- Manual campaign structure built over 4 years losing ground to algorithm-native competitors
- iOS 14.5 hangover: pixel-based retargeting audiences shrunk materially
- Attribution stack gap: Northbeam showed growing gap between modeled và reported conversions
- Rigid segmentation starving Meta's ML layer — "fighting the algorithm at every turn"

> "We were essentially telling Meta's machine learning to stay in its lane — and Meta's machine learning stopped caring what we thought."  
> — Christiane Lemieux, Growth Advisor

---

## Advantage+ Full Migration Setup

### Core Changes
1. **Consolidate 14 ad sets → 3 ASC campaigns:** (1) New customer acquisition, (2) Lapsed purchaser reactivation, (3) Upsell to existing buyers
2. **Seeding CRM data từ Klaviyo:** suppression lists, high-LTV profiles, 90-day purchaser windows → upload as custom audiences
3. **Creative volume tăng từ 8 → 31 assets** — static product photography, lifestyle UGC, founder-led video, comparison carousels
4. **Bỏ manual bid caps** → cost-per-result goals calibrated với 60-day LTV window (không phải 7-day click ROAS)
5. **Weekly creative refresh:** retire asset có frequency >3.2 trong 14 ngày

### Transition Period
- 6 weeks structured migration (không cold-turkey switch)
- First 2 weeks felt like "free-falling" — ROAS dip trước khi stabilize
- Cần budget buffer 15-20% cho transition period

> "Advantage+ doesn't eliminate strategy — it elevates the inputs that matter. Creative quality and first-party data seeding became the only dials we had left."  
> — Danielle Park, Head of Performance Marketing

## Kịch bản A: Advantage+ Full Migration (Đã chứng minh)

**Setup:** 3 ASC campaigns + CRM seeding + creative matrix + weekly refresh  
**Best for:** DTC brands với $2M+/tháng spend, đã có pixel data, muốn scale nhanh  
**Risk:** Free-falling phase 2 tuần đầu → cần budget buffer và team prepared cho transition

## Kịch bản B: Creative Matrix Approach (Đã chứng minh)

**Setup — 5 format creative matrix:**
1. **Problem-aware static ads** (top funnel, cold audience) → address kitchen clutter & PTFE concerns
2. **Founder-led 30-second videos** (mid funnel, brand building) → material science story
3. **UGC testimonial compilations** (full funnel, trust signal) → real customers in real kitchens
4. **Comparison carousels** (bottom funnel, consideration) → benchmark vs legacy brands on health/durability/aesthetics
5. **Seasonal product spotlights** (gifting moments + seasonal cooking events)

**Insight:** "The system rewards creative diversity — not just creative quantity. It needed different emotional registers, different hooks, different lengths. Build a matrix, not a backlog."  
— Marcus Holloway, Creative Director

## Kịch bản C: First-Party Data Seeding Strategy (Đã chứng minh)

**Setup — 6 Klaviyo segments làm value-based custom audiences:**
1. Subscribers purchased 2x trong 90 days
2. Single-purchase buyers AOV >$180
3. Lapsed customers last active 120-180 days ago
4. Product category cohort: cookware sets
5. Product category cohort: bakeware
6. Product category cohort: storage

**Results:**
- CAC Advantage+ thấp hơn manual **34%**
- 90-day LTV của Advantage+ customers cao hơn **18%**
- Algorithm found people brand "never would have targeted manually" — heterogeneous group converts at better rate

**Phản biện:** CRM seeding chỉ effective khi data quality cao. Dirty email lists → noisy match scores → algorithm confused. Cần data hygiene trước khi upload.

---

## Bài Học Key

1. **Consolidation = faster learning.** 14 ad sets → 3 campaigns = algorithm có đủ signal để optimize
2. **Creative diversity > creative quantity** — different emotional registers, hooks, lengths trong matrix approach
3. **First-party data seeding là input quan trọng nhất cho Advantage+** — Klaviyo segments treated as paid media targeting input
4. **LTV-calibrated cost goals (60-day) tốt hơn 7-day click ROAS** — tránh buying one-time purchasers
5. **Transition period cần buffer** — free-falling phase 2 tuần đầu là normal, không panic-edit
