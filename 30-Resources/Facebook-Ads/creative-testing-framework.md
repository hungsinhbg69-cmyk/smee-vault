## CREATIVE TESTING PIPELINE (4 Bước)

### Step 1: Competitor Audit
- Search Facebook Ad Library → identify ads running ≥30 ngày trong category
- Note: hook structures, offer framings, visual treatments, CTAs
- Goal: Identify what's working (long-running = profitable)

### Step 2: Variant Matrix Design
```
Hook angles (3): Angle A / Angle B / Angle C
× Visual treatments (2): UGC style / Produced quality  
× CTAs (2): Soft / Direct
= 12 variants per creative brief minimum
```

### Step 3: Test Execution
- **Minimum:** 1,000-2,000 impressions + 7 days trước khi scale/kill
- **Statistical significance:** ≥50 conversions cho metric confidence
- **One variable at a time** — isolate what works
- Document all results (hook rate, CTR, CPA, ROAS per variant)

### Step 4: Scale & Refresh Cycle
- Winners → scale via horizontal duplication or ≤20% vertical increase every 48h
- Losers → kill immediately (don't salvage underperforming creatives)
- **Refresh cadence:** Every 10-14 days proactively, don't wait for collapse
- Creative lifespan on Meta: 3-6 weeks maximum

## HOOK-RATE BENCHMARKS (Critical Metric)

| Score | Level | Action |
|-------|-------|--------|
| <25% | Weak | Rewrite hook — pattern interrupt not strong enough |
| 25-30% | Solid | Keep testing variations, monitor CTR correlation |
| 30-40% | Good | Scale this angle, create derivative variants |
| 40%+ | Elite | Double down — foundation for winning creative genome |

**Hook Rate = (3-second video views / Impressions) × 100**

---

## HOOK FRAMEWORKS (Proven Structures)

### Pattern Interrupt Hooks
- "I tried [X] marketing tools and here's what happened..."
- "This mistake cost me $[amount]..."
- Stop scrolling hooks: visual disruption in first frame

### Problem-Solution Hooks  
- "Tired of [pain point]? Here's the fix..."
- Show transformation before/after visually

### Social Proof Hooks
- "[Number] people already using this — here's why"
- Customer testimonial opening line

## CREATIVE FATIGUE DETECTION

| Metric | Signal | Action Threshold |
|--------|--------|------------------|
| Frequency >3.0 | Audience saturation | Immediate refresh |
| CTR falling 15%+ from baseline | Ad blindness | Within 48h test new hooks |
| CPA increasing 20%+ from baseline | Conversion fatigue | Hold 3-4 days → refresh creative |
| Daily impressions dropping while budget constant | Delivery efficiency loss | Check relevance diagnostics |

**Fast pacing + rapid frequency increase = imminent creative fatigue** — watch BDV (Budget Deployment Velocity) alongside frequency.

## VIETNAM CREATIVE ADJUSTMENTS

- **UGC performs exceptionally well** — Vietnamese audiences trust peer recommendations over polished ads
- **Video-first strategy** — Reels/short-form video dominates feed engagement
- **Language:** Mix of Vietnamese + English tech terms works better than pure Vietnamese for B2B/DTC
- **Payment trust signals:** COD badge, "thanh toán khi nhận hàng" in creatives increases conversion
- **Zalo cross-channel:** Meta ad → Zalo conversation closing funnel
