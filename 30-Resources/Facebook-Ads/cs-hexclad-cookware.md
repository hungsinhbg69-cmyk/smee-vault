# Hexclad — Concept-First Testing Framework

## Dữ Liệu Thực Tế

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| ROAS (Meta) | 2.4x | **3.8x** | ✅ +58% |
| CPA | $88 | **$61** | ✅ -31% |
| Annual Spend | Baseline | **>$40M** | ✅ |

**Ngân sách:** $25K-$100K+/tháng  
**Industry:** Premium Cookware (Gordon Ramsay brand)  
**Duration:** 18-month rebuild of creative testing infrastructure  
**Goal:** Restructure campaign architecture, overhaul creative brief system, layer new attribution methodology

---

## Problem Ban Đầu

- Running **60-70 creative variants** at a time, calling it "testing" — nhưng không có structured hypothesis
- Optimizing wrong signals at wrong stage of funnel
- Meta in-platform ROAS 3.1x vs true blended 2.1x → gap nearly full turn driven by retargeting cannibalization
- Aggressive retargeting: 30-day window, high frequency → hammering small pool of users

> "We were running 60, 70 creative variants at a time and calling it testing. But we didn't have a structured hypothesis behind any of it."  
> — Daniel Park, VP of Growth

---

## Core Rebuild Components

### 1. Concept-First Testing Framework
- Giảm từ 60-70 variants → chỉ **6-8 distinct concepts per cycle**
- Mỗi concept = single hypothesis cụ thể (NOT "Gordon Ramsay uses this")
- Isolation: mỗi concept own ad set controlled budget → eliminate winner-take-all dynamic
- Signal layering: Meta in-platform + Northbeam cross-channel + post-purchase survey question
- Velocity gates: **minimum 40 attributed purchases** trước khi scale

### 2. Incrementality-Led Retargeting
- Meta Conversion Lift test: **60% retargeting conversions** là people sẽ purchase anyway trong 72h
- Retargeting window cắt từ 30 ngày → 7 ngày cho warm audiences
- Frequency caps: **3 impressions/user/week**
- Retargeting budget reallocate sang prospecting (stronger incremental lift)

### 3. Three-Tier Creative System
- Tier 1 Rapid UGC: lo-fi mobile content, top funnel prospecting ($400/video)
- Tier 2 Hybrid editorial: athlete footage + data-forward copy overlays, mid-funnel
- Tier 3 Studio anchor: 2-3 high-production brand films/quarter, retargeting/brand reinforcement

## Kịch bản A: Concept-First Testing Framework (Đã chứng minh)

**Setup:**
- Giảm từ 60-70 variants → chỉ 6-8 distinct concepts per cycle
- Mỗi concept = single hypothesis cụ thể (ví dụ: "A pan that works same after 200 washes as day one")
- Isolation: mỗi concept own ad set controlled budget
- Signal layering: Meta + Northbeam + post-purchase survey
- Velocity gates: minimum 40 attributed purchases trước khi scale

**Insight:** "We were spending $40,000 on a video that a creator could beat with a $400 shoot." — Tier 1 UGC consistently outperforms high-production trên prospecting ROAS.

**Rachel Jimenez (Sharma Brands):** "Best creative comes from actual customer language — specific, weird phrases people use when explaining why they bought something. That's your hook."

## Kịch bản B: Incrementality-Led Retargeting (Đã chứng minh)

**Setup:**
- Meta Conversion Lift test → reveal 60% retargeting waste
- Retargeting window: 30 ngày → 7 ngày cho warm audiences
- Frequency caps: 3 impressions/user/week
- Budget reallocate sang prospecting
- Separate creative cho retargeting: shorter, more direct, no brand education

**Daniel Park:** "The incrementality test was the most uncomfortable thing we did in 2025. It told us we'd been wasting a meaningful percentage of our retargeting budget for years."

## Kịch bản C: Three-Tier Creative System (Đã chứng minh)

**Setup — 3 tier creative production:**

| Tier | Format | Cost | Purpose | Funnel Stage |
|------|--------|------|---------|--------------|
| **Tier 1: Rapid UGC** | Lo-fi mobile content | ~$400/video | Top funnel prospecting | Awareness → Consideration |
| **Tier 2: Hybrid Editorial** | Athlete footage + data-forward copy overlays | Mid-range | Mid-funnel trust building | Consideration |
| **Tier 3: Studio Anchor** | 2-3 high-production brand films/quarter | High ($10K+) | Retargeting/brand reinforcement | Conversion |

**Key insight:** Tier 1 content cost fraction of studio production nhưng consistently outperform trên prospecting ROAS. Shift from "polished studio production as default" → UGC-native cho top funnel.

---

## Bài Học Key

1. **60-70 variants ≠ testing.** Concept-first framework với 6-8 concepts + hypothesis rõ ràng > volume vô tội vạ
2. **Incrementality testing = non-negotiable** — Conversion Lift reveal 60% retargeting waste → reallocate budget sang prospecting
3. **$40K studio video beaten by $400 UGC shoot** — Tier 1 rapid UGC consistently outperform trên prospecting ROAS
4. **Velocity gates (40 purchases) reduce false positives** — trade-off giữa velocity và accuracy trong testing decisions
5. **Customer language > agency trends** — 20-minute customer interviews identify specific, weird phrases = best hooks
