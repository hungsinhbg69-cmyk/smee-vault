# AI Agent Marketing Architecture

> Reference concept for understanding how AI agents drive modern marketing operations. Resolves links to agent-integration-framework and facebook-algorithm-needs-volume.

## Key Principles
1. **First-party data as primary asset** — AI agents leverage owned audience data (email lists, pixel events, CRM) to create lookalike signals
2. **Creative as the primary targeting lever** — in 2026+, creative quality drives algorithmic optimization more than audience segmentation
3. **Agent-augmented workflows** — automated capture → connect → decide → ship cycle reduces human touchpoints by 70%+ on routine marketing ops

### Agent Architecture Layers
- **Capture Layer:** Web scraping, social listening, content ingestion into vault/resources
- **Connect Layer:** Backlinking, cross-referencing, knowledge synthesis
- **Decide Layer:** Performance analysis, campaign optimization, budget allocation
- **Ship Layer:** Content publication, ad deployment, report generation
