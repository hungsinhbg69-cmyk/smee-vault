# High-Ticket Home Improvement — ROAS 1.18 → 6.47

## Dữ Liệu Thực Tế

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| ROAS (Before) | 1.18 |
| ROAS (After) | **6.47** |
| Revenue Generated | **$700,000+** |
| Duration | **120 ngày** |
| Product Price Range | $1,000 - $5,000 (đến cửa, cửa sổ) |

**Ngân sách:** $3K-$25K/tháng  
**Industry:** Home Improvement — premium doors & windows  
**Model transition:** Lead-gen-only → DTC e-commerce powerhouse  
**Goal:** Shift customer behavior từ "calling to ask" → "clicking to buy"

---

## Challenges Ban Đầu

- Zero funnel structure: Meta Ads chạy không retargeting hoặc segmentation
- Algorithm mismanagement: Meta autopilot dump budget vào older Facebook placements, neglect Instagram audiences
- High friction: delivery zones confusion, product dimensions uncertainty → drop-off cao
- Low trust: high-ticket items require multiple touchpoints, brand lacked nurturing system
- Historically "brick-and-click" model — website chỉ để generate phone calls

---

## Core Strategy — The Growth Method

### Phase 1: Strategic Analysis & Quick Wins
- Activate retargeting layer cho website visitors + users initiated calls but haven't purchased
- Introduce **"Buy Online & Save 10%"** offer → tangible reason skip phone call, head straight to checkout

### Phase 2: Identify & Eliminate Purchase Barriers
- GA4 analysis + call-center feedback identify 2 major conversion killers:
  - **Delivery uncertainty:** Deploy creatives map delivery timelines, zones, fees
  - **Sizing anxiety:** "Measure Your Door" guides + education-based creatives

### Phase 3: Segmentation for Scalable ROAS
- Data show profitability concentrated trong **5 states** → tighten geo-fencing
- Age 45+ isolated vào standalone campaigns (high-ticket demographic phù hợp)
- Instagram-only prospecting feed top funnel fresh high-CTR traffic

---

## Kịch bản A: 3-Step Retargeting Funnel (Đã chứng minh)

### Setup — Structured Narrative Follow User Journey

| Phase | Trigger | Creative | Offer |
|-------|---------|----------|-------|
| **Phase 1: Education** | All website visitors | Product value + trust signals + delivery transparency | No discount |
| **Phase 2: The Hook** | Engaged users chưa purchase | "Buy Online & Save 10%" | **10% discount** |
| **Phase 3: The Closer** | Multiple visits OR cart items | Exclusive "Last Call" urgency | **15% discount** |

**Data insight:** High-ticket home goods cần ~7 touchpoints để convert. Structured narrative guide user qua journey thay vì single conversion event.

**Phân tích:** "Buy Online & Save 10%" offer shift customer behavior từ "calling to ask" → "clicking to buy". Đây không chỉ là creative strategy mà là behavioral economics nudge.

### Kịch bản B: Geo-Fencing + Demographic Isolation (Đã chứng minh)

**Setup:**
- GA4 data show profitability concentrated trong 5 states → tighten geo-fencing
- Age 45+ isolated vào standalone campaigns
- Instagram-only prospecting feed top funnel fresh high-CTR traffic
- Scale budget trong high-performing states không làm collapse ROAS

**Results:**
- Phase 1 (2 tháng đầu): ROAS stabilize above 4.0
- Phase 2 (tháng 4): **542% increase** total purchase value so với starting period

**Phản biện:** Geo-fencing quá tight → limited scale ceiling. Balance giữa efficiency (high-performing states) và scale (expand to new states khi ROAS stable >3x).

### Kịch bản C: Full-Funnel Creative Overhaul (Đã chứng minh)

**Setup — Creative Types:**
1. **Problem-solving videos:** Direct answers sizing/installation fears
2. **UGC walkthroughs:** Real customers show quality doors in homes → trust signal cho high-ticket
3. **Angle-based testing:** "Best-Seller" compilations vs educational visuals → identify highest intent driver

**Barrier-fix creatives cụ thể:**
- Delivery uncertainty: Explicit maps delivery timelines, zones, fees
- Sizing anxiety: "Measure Your Door" guides + education-based content
- Trust signals: Installation process videos, warranty info, customer testimonials

**Phân tích:** High-ticket items ($1K-$5K) require multiple touchpoints. Static catalog images insufficient → cần narrative creative build trust và消除 purchase barriers.

---

## Bài Học Key

1. **High-ticket items = ~7 touchpoints để convert.** Structured retargeting funnel với progressive offers (education → 10% discount → 15% closer)
2. **"Buy Online & Save X%" nudge** shift customer behavior từ phone call → online purchase
3. **Geo-fencing + demographic isolation** = scale profitable markets trước khi expand
4. **Full-funnel creative > static catalog images** cho high-ticket — problem-solving videos, UGC walkthroughs, angle-based testing
5. **120 ngày transformation 1.18→6.47 ROAS** chứng minh high-ticket có thể sell effectively qua structured Meta ecosystem
